About Ben

Ben Cochrane-Cole is a known rapist with a history of manipulative, misogynistic, and violent behavior. This website was started by a group of his victims who are seeking to shine light on his history, and to prevent him from victimizing others any more.

Ben is currently applying to law schools and positions in the legal field. We urge you - do not admit him into your programs or your practice. Consistently throughout his undergraduate career, he has used his “interest” in representing victims of sexual violence as a way to have us let our guard down around him, giving him the chance to isolate us and sexually assault us. Ben is the embodiment of the predatory behavior he claims to work against.

We have tried going to the police, the courts, and his schools, trying to put an end to this behavior. We have been told there’s no shot at justice, that his parents hold too much power in the Hennepin County and Minnesota justice system, and that they will spare no expense to protect their beloved son. Some victims have reported being followed and harassed by private investigators to prevent us from coming forward. We are currently working with the FBI to explore the option of a federal case against him.

Since launching this project in 2016, we have been contacted by and shared our experiences with:

  • 13 women who have been sexually assaulted by Ben

  • 6 men who have been sexually assaulted by Ben

  • 2 nonbinary/genderqueer individuals who have been sexually assaulted by Ben

  • 26 women and 19 men with whom Ben has gotten intoxicated and attempted to assault

  • 79 women and 12 men who have been sexually harassed and made uncomfortable by Ben

  • 9 victims of other violent crimes, 15 victims of property crimes, and 31 people defrauded by Ben in his role as a drug dealer.
